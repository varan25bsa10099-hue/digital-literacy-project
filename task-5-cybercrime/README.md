### 🔍 Part A: Cybercrime Case Study
**Topic:** RTO E-Challan Phishing Scam (Gondia/Bhandara Incident)

* **The Incident:** A victim lost ₹5,00,000 after clicking a malicious link disguised as an official traffic fine notification from the RTO.
* **Mechanism (Modus Operandi):** * **Social Engineering:** Exploiting fear of legal penalties.
    * **Smishing:** Sending fraudulent SMS with "spoofed" URLs.
    * **Credential Harvesting:** Stealing UPI/Banking data via fake portals.
* **Key Learning:** Technical security is ineffective if users are not trained to spot "urgent" social engineering tactics.

---

### 🛡️ Part B: Stay Safe Online Checklist
To combat the threats identified in Part A, I developed a localized safety checklist featuring 8 actionable tips:

1. **Financial Security:** The "Golden Rule"—A UPI PIN is **only** for sending money, never for receiving.
2. **Technical Hardening:** Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and App Permission audits.
3. **Domain Verification:** Identifying official government portals via `.gov.in` extensions.
4. **Emergency Channels:** Instant reporting via **1930** and **cybercrime.gov.in**.
