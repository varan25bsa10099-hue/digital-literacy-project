# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online: A Digital Hygiene Checklist

### **General Digital Safety Tips**

* **1. Strengthen Passwords:** Use a unique, complex passphrase for your student portals and personal emails. Avoid using your date of birth or "123456."

* **2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Activate 2FA on WhatsApp, Instagram, and LinkedIn to prevent account hijacking, even if your password is leaked.

* **3. Audit App Permissions:** Regularly check which apps have access to your microphone, camera, and contacts. Delete unnecessary "utility" apps that may contain adware.

* **4. Verify Official Domains:** Always check for **.gov.in** or **.nic.in** when accessing government services like RTO, Aadhaar, or University portals.

* **5. Secure Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid logging into banking apps while using free public Wi-Fi at airports or cafes; use mobile data instead.

* **6. Think Before You Click:** Hover over links in emails or SMS to see the actual destination. If it looks like a string of random characters, do not click it.

### **Financial & UPI Safety**

* **7. The 'Receive' Rule:** Remember that **entering your UPI PIN is only for sending money**, never for receiving it. Scanning to "receive" is a common scam.

* **8. Verify UPI IDs:** Before hitting 'Pay' for college fests or vendors, double-check the verified name displayed on the UPI app to ensure it matches.

---

### **Emergency Reporting Channels 📞**

* **National Cyber Crime Helpline:** Dial **1930** immediately.
* **Official Reporting Portal:** [www.cybercrime.gov.in](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in)
* **Bank Alert:** Contact your bank’s fraud division within the "Golden Hour" to freeze transactions.
